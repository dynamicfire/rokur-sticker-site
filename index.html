English

Rokur Sticker Pack

An iMessage sticker pack featuring Rokur (玄昼), an original furry character based on the Mountain Bull Red Four-Eyed Tusong dog — a rare native Chinese breed.

Support

Found a bug, want to report a sticker that isn't displaying, or have feedback? The fastest way to reach us is by filing an issue on GitHub:

  • Open a new issue

Privacy

Rokur Sticker Pack does not collect any user data. The app is purely a static set of image stickers — there are no servers, no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising. The stickers you send remain part of your iMessage conversations and are subject to Apple's standard Messages privacy policy.

隐私政策

Rokur Sticker Pack 不收集任何用户数据。本 App 是纯静态的贴纸资源——不连接任何服务器，不做数据分析、不追踪、不投放广告。你发送的贴纸仅作为 iMessage 对话的一部分存在，遵循 Apple 标准的 Messages 隐私政策。
